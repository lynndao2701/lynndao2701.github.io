## Overview

This project analysed the inventory management failures that contributed to Target Canada's collapse, and designed a Business Intelligence solution to address the root causes. The goal was to demonstrate how a BI-enhanced ERP framework could have prevented stockouts, overstocking, and poor decision-making by centralising data and enabling real-time visibility.

## What I Did

- Conducted a current-state process review to identify root causes of inventory management failures and delayed decision-making
- Centralised data from ERP, POS, and warehouse systems to provide accurate inventory visibility
- Designed interactive **Power BI dashboards** to support real-time tracking and managerial decision-making
- Applied **machine learning demand forecasting** concepts to improve stock prediction and reduce manual forecasting errors
- Produced recommendations addressing data quality risks, adaptive forecasting, and inventory control improvements aligned to measurable operational outcomes

## Outcomes

- BI Solutions and Interactive Dashboard designs addressing real-time inventory visibility
- ERP improvement framework with centralised data architecture
- Recommendations targeting prevention of stockouts, overstocking, and delayed responses to market changes

## Skills Used

Business Intelligence (BI), ERP Analysis, Data Warehousing, ELT, Dashboard Design, Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Machine Learning Concepts, Data Security Analysis, Root Cause Analysis, Process Review, Business Documentation
