## Overview

During my internship with the Newcastle Knights Commercial Team, I was engaged as a Business and Market Research Analyst to research and develop a strategic Business Club proposal. The goal was to identify a commercially viable membership model that would improve corporate engagement and retention over a three-year period.

## What I Did

- Benchmarked 20+ business membership programs across 11 NRL and AFL clubs to identify pricing strategies and competitive gaps
- Conducted market analysis and segmented target audiences by geography, industry, and behaviour
- Analysed pricing, membership, and engagement data using **Power BI** and **Excel** to surface market gaps and inform program design
- Designed a year-round engagement model featuring VIP hospitality, networking, and professional development events
- Developed a **3-year financial forecast** and defined KPIs for customer satisfaction, sales performance, and marketing effectiveness
- Delivered a structured proposal covering objectives, target market, pricing strategy, success measures, and brand exposure opportunities

## Outcomes

- Proposed engagement model projected to improve **corporate member retention and engagement by 20%**
- Accelerated identification of a commercially suitable program structure by **30%** through detailed competitor benchmarking and strategic evaluation
- Delivered a full evidence-based proposal ready for commercial review

## Skills Used

Business Requirements Gathering, Stakeholder Analysis, Competitor Benchmarking, Market Research, Data Analysis, Power BI, Excel (Power Query, DAX), Pricing Strategy, Financial Forecasting, KPI Development, Business Documentation, Strategic Planning Support
