## Overview

This project explored how Natural Language Processing (NLP) can transform unstructured customer feedback into structured, decision-ready business insights. Using a Python-based pipeline, I applied text preprocessing, sentiment analysis, and LDA topic modelling to over 1,000 Apple customer reviews to identify recurring themes, sentiment trends, and product improvement opportunities.

## What I Did

- Built a **Python-based NLP pipeline** covering text cleaning, tokenisation, lemmatisation, and stop word removal
- Applied **sentiment analysis** to classify reviews as positive, negative, or neutral and identify sentiment trends by product feature
- Used **LDA topic modelling** to surface key themes including product quality, battery life, and usability
- Produced **word clouds, bigram/trigram charts**, and visualisations to make findings accessible to non-technical stakeholders
- Documented ethical considerations around data privacy, algorithmic bias, and responsible AI use in business decision-making

## Outcomes

- Customer feedback analysis across 1,000+ reviews
- Sentiment classification and trend identification by product theme
- Topic modelling revealing key product concerns and feature feedback
- Insight report and visualisations ready for business decision support

## Skills Used

Python, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Text Analytics, Tokenisation, Lemmatisation, Sentiment Analysis, LDA Topic Modelling, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Visualisation, Word Cloud Analysis, Bigram and Trigram Analysis, Business Insight Reporting
