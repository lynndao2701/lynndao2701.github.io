## Overview

TripMate is a smart travel ecosystem concept that addresses the fragmented nature of current travel planning. This project applied design thinking, business process modelling, and stakeholder analysis to map the current-state pain points and design a future-state integrated platform — including a high-fidelity Figma prototype and a multi-sided business model.

## What I Did

- Applied **design thinking and empathy mapping** to identify pain points in fragmented travel planning journeys
- Conducted **stakeholder analysis** to understand the needs of travellers, local businesses, and event organisers
- Produced a structured **AS-IS process analysis** identifying current inefficiencies across booking, transport, dining, and accommodation planning
- Designed a **TO-BE process model using BPMN** to map the future-state integrated travel platform
- Delivered a **high-fidelity Figma prototype** demonstrating AI-powered itinerary generation, booking integration, transport coordination, and loyalty features
- Developed a **multi-sided business model** with a revenue model covering commissions, subscriptions, advertising, and loyalty partnerships
- Presented findings to a stakeholder audience with supporting business documentation

## Outcomes

- AS-IS process analysis of fragmented travel planning
- TO-BE BPMN process design for an integrated smart travel ecosystem
- High-fidelity Figma prototype
- Multi-sided platform business model and revenue model
- Joint value proposition for travellers, local businesses, and event organisers
